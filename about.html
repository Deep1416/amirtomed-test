About Amritomed

Amritomed Pharmaceutical improves healthcare with innovative medicines, scientific excellence, and patient-focused solutions worldwide.

Founded in January 2023, Amritomed Pharmaceutical Private Limited is a New Delhi-based company committed to transforming healthcare. Currently, we specialize in gynecology and obstetrics, with plans to expand into pediatrics soon.
As a caring and integrated pharmaceutical company, we are dedicated to improving human health through the discovery, development, and commercialization of innovative medicines. Registered as a Non-Government Company under the Registrar of Companies (ROC), Delhi, Amritomed strives to uphold the highest standards of scientific excellence and healthcare innovation.
At Amritomed, we are driven by purpose—to make a real difference in people’s lives. Our team of passionate healthcare professionals works tirelessly to develop groundbreaking medicines that tackle some of the world's most challenging health concerns. We prioritize patients above all, delivering cutting-edge solutions that enhance their quality of life and well-being.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in the healthcare industry by delivering innovative, high-quality, and affordable medicines that improve patient lives. We are committed to ethical practices, sustainable development, and social responsibility

Our Mission

Our mission is to make a powerful impact in the pharmaceutical industry by achieving a pan-India presence within two years, ensuring accessibility and trust across the nation. In the next five years, we aim to expand globally, establishing our footprint in international markets. Simultaneously, we are committed to securing a place among India’s top pharmaceutical companies, driven by innovation, excellence, and an unwavering commitment to quality healthcare.

Our belivies

We believe in fostering strong human connections, driving sustainable growth, and balancing business with humanity. Our commitment to a positive work culture enhances collaboration between employees and management. We celebrate milestones, embrace innovation, and continuously develop new strategies to achieve our goals, creating more opportunities for growth.

Our Team

At Amritomed Pharmaceutical Private Limited, we are driven by a passionate and highly experienced team that is dedicated to making a real impact in the healthcare industry. Our team’s mission is to develop life-saving and life-changing medications, directly contributing to improving human health and enhancing patients' quality of life.
We take pride in having an excellent and experienced team, led by industry veterans with over 20 years of expertise in the pharmaceutical sector. Our team leaders have worked with some of India's top pharmaceutical companies, bringing a wealth of knowledge, innovation, and leadership to Amritomed. Their vast experience enables us to maintain the highest standards in research, development, and healthcare solutions.
Additionally, we are committed to continuous learning and professional growth. We implement and evaluate programs that empower our team members, ensuring they stay at the forefront of pharmaceutical advancements. Through skill development and ongoing training, we cultivate a dynamic work environment where innovation thrives, and every individual contributes to our shared mission of transforming healthcare.
